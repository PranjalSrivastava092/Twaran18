About Us

"Winning ain't Everything...it's the only thing!"

"The roar of the champions and the cheer of the audience”-are what we get to hear in the season of Spring embarking the start of our NATIONAL SPORTS FEST “TWARAN”.

Twaran is a 3 day extravaganza of sports held under the flag of ABV-IIITM to provide a platform for students from institutes all over the country to exhibit their marvellous talent and skills by competing with the best upcoming sports persons in the country in a superbly charged and competitive ambience with state-of-art sports facilities.

“Success isn’t given. It’s Earned. On the track, on the field, In the Gym. With Blood, Sweat, And the occasional Tear.”

Let’s play to strengthen our bonds, to become a symbol of friendship and unity, to witness those heart stopping crucial moments of the game and most importantly let’s play to increase the mutual understanding and to foster cooperation level among various institutes so that we stand together united. A majority of the games are held under floodlights including basketball, volleyball, table tennis and badminton. Several others like cricket, football, lawn tennis take place on the lush green fields during the day. Informal events like carom, chess and marathon add exuberance to the heated environment of Twaran.
